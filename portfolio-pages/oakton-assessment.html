Learning Outcome Assessment

  • 2014-2015
  • Assessment

Project Info

As a relatively new office, the office on New Student Experiences had a heavy emphasis on assessment of learning outcomes. While working there, I thus completed a comprehensive learning outcome assessment map including the following tasks:

  • Establishing desired learning outcomes for new students, parents, and student peer leaders.
  • Banchmarking learning outcomes to local and national New Student Orientation Outcomes based on best practices research from the National Orientation Directors Association (NODA) and the Oakton General Learning Outcomes.
  • Determined and implemented assessment methods for learning outcomes.
  • Compiled and presented data on learning outcomes in a poster session (see live preview) and presentation.

Before engaging in this project, I had never completed a learning outcome assessment plan before. As such, I learned how to establish an assessment map, create assessment methods and benchmarks, and ultimately assess the outcomes against those established assessment methods. This process taught me a great deal about how to measure information about program outcomes and to present that data in a way that is useful for administrators to use in making informed decisions.
