MSHE Virtual Student Orientation

  • 2014-2015
  • Web Design, ELearning, Assessment

Project Info

New student virtual orientation was designed and conceived from scratch in order to provide a seamless onboarding experience for incoming students in the Master of Science in Higher Education Administration and Policy program and Northwestern University. After estalishing information priorities for incoming students, we designed a comprehensive module-based online orientation which supported students in their academic preparation, policies, financial aid, housing and relocation experience, and introduction to student life on and off campus through a combination of videos, wrtten content, and interactive matieral. Producing the virtual orientation included:

  • Filming, producing, and editing over 15 interviews with current students faculty and staff members as the core component of the orientation.
  • Creating interactive software demonstrations for teaching the Canvas LMS, course registration, course planning, and financial aid systems.
  • Establishing methods of building and supporting online community through discussion boards and social media integration for content.
  • And, finally, building assessment techniques through a combination of assessment questions and completion benchmarks.
