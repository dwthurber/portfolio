Virtual Tutor Training

  • 2013-2014
  • ELearning, Web Design

Project Info

The comprehensive online training modules were designed completely by me for the tutor training program at Riverland Community College as part of my AmeriCorps VISTA project. When I came to Riverland, the school was rapidly expanding to more online instruction and the tutoring program was expanding to adapt. This online training was built using SmartBuilder as a fully SCORM 1.4 and 2.0 compliant program to train tutors in the same comprehensive training program designed by me (more details on the training manual project page). The online training included among other things:

  • Interactive game-based learning modules.
  • Responsive virtual tutoring simulation environment.
  • Comprehensive modules structure.
  • Universal design concepts for accessibility compliance.
